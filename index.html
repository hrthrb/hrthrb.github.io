About the Author

I’m Caitriona, a philosopher-in-progress with a passion for untangling life's most intricate questions – and tying them into even more knots. My work explores the philosophical undercurrents of everything from abstract theoretics to everyday experience, treating philosophy not just as an academic pursuit but as a living dialogue – weaving together classical ideas with personal insight.

Here you’ll find my collected works in progress. Most of what I share will be assignments from school – essays, reflections, and whatever else the academic world throws my way. While not every piece may begin as a work of philosophy, I bring a philosophical lens to everything I write. Philosophy isn’t just a subject for me – it’s the way I engage with the world, and Fragments of Thought & Self is where that engagement takes shape.
